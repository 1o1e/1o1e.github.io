A few of us have been talking about starting a regular in-person meetup for local developers. Here's what we're working with.

<!-- truncate -->

## Why bother

Online communities are great but there's something that only happens when people are in the same room. Lower barrier to random conversation, faster trust-building, and honestly — it's just more fun.

## Format ideas

- **Lightning talks** — 5–10 min, no slides required, just show something you built or learned
- **Open hacking** — show up, work on something, ask for help if you need it
- **Theme nights** — one topic (e.g., databases, compilers, side projects) with 1–2 speakers

## What we need

- A venue (café, coworking space, office with a projector)
- A loose cadence — monthly seems manageable
- A few people willing to talk the first time

## Status

We have a group chat and a tentative first date. If you're in the area and interested, reach out.
